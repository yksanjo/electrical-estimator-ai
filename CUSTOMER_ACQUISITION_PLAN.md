## Conversion Optimization

### Pricing Strategy for First 10 Customers
**Option 1: Founder's Plan**
- First 10 customers: $49/month (50% off) for life
- Create urgency: "Only 3 spots left at founder's pricing"

**Option 2: Free First Month**
- No credit card required
- After 30 days: $99/month
- Easier conversion, but lower commitment

**Option 3: Annual Discount**
- $99/month OR $999/year (2 months free)
- Gets cash upfront
- Higher commitment

**Recommended**: Start with Option 2, then switch to Option 1 after first 3 customers

## Scripts & Templates

### Discovery Call Script (15 minutes)

**1. Introduction (2 min)**
"Thanks for taking the time. Before I show you the platform, tell me about your current estimating process?"

**2. Pain Discovery (3 min)**
- How long does a typical estimate take?
- What's the most frustrating part?
- What happens if you miss something in the takeoff?
- Have you tried other estimating software?

**3. Demo (5 min)**
- Show dashboard
- Walk through estimate creation
- Highlight time savings
- Show material list output

**4. Trial Close (3 min)**
"Based on what you've seen, does this solve the problem you mentioned?"

**5. Handle Objections (2 min)**
[Use objection handling from above]

**6. Close**
"I'd love to have you as a founding customer. First month is free, no credit card needed. Can I set you up with an account right now?"

## Alternative: Bootstrap vs. Wait for Full Product

### Option A: Get Customers NOW (Recommended)
- Offer "blueprint estimating service" manually
- Charge $99/month for 10 estimates
- YOU do the analysis initially
- Use this to:
  1. Get revenue immediately
  2. Understand customer needs deeply
  3. Build perfect AI integration later
  4. Already have customers when AI is ready

### Option B: Wait Until Full Product
- Add OpenAI, Stripe, S3 first
- Delay revenue by 2-4 weeks
- Risk: Building features customers might not want
- Risk: Burning time without validation

**Recommended**: Start with Option A for first 3 customers, then switch to automated product
